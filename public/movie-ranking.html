The Best Movies According to Abinav

A Few Favorites from My Mind

The Shawshank Redemption

A powerful story of hope, friendship, and freedom. It's one of those movies that leave
a lasting impression on your soul.

PK

A unique take on society and faith with a mix of humor and emotions. Aamir Khan’s
performance is truly unforgettable.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

A beautiful journey of friendship, adventure, and finding yourself. It’s one of those
movies that you can watch again and again.

Gully Boy

A raw and inspiring story about chasing your dreams, with brilliant performances
and an amazing soundtrack.

Drishyam

A gripping thriller with clever twists and an intense storyline that keeps you hooked
till the very end.
